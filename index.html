Cities I Have Lived In:

I have lived in a variety of places during my time as a military brat, as well as my own military service, both domestic and abroad. Some of these places include the following:

  • Colorado Springs, CO: Not only do I have family here, but I was stationed in Colorado Springs for several years.
  • Wichita Falls, TX: I spent time here as a child while my parents were stationed at the local AF Base.
  • Tacoma, WA: I loved the green, beautiful parks near Tacoma; I was also stationed here.
  • Vilnius, LT: I lived in the capitol city of Lithuania for 6 months. It was an incredible experience with amazing opportunities.
  • Bydgoszsc, PL: I lived in this small Polish city in a local apartment while working with local military forces; I made some incredible friends.
